No surprises

The fine print,
in plain English.

Board is due the 1st. A $25 late fee kicks in on the 6th — it's in the contract, not a surprise.

Every horse arrives with a signed boarding agreement, current Coggins, and a waiver. No exceptions, even for friends. Especially for friends — that's a lesson we already learned so you don't have to.

Farrier and vet passthroughs are billed the same week at cost plus $10. Rate changes come with 30 days written notice. We review rates every January.

What pasture board is not: training rides, show prep, or mane pulling. We offer all of it — with a price attached — just ask.
