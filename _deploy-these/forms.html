Forms · paperwork

Sign it
once.
On your phone.

Every form below fills out in a few minutes — no printing, no scanning. Hit submit and it lands with us automatically and is filed for keeps.

Not sure which you need? Boarders sign the waiver + boarding agreement. Lesson riders sign the lesson registration (which includes the waiver). Horses in for training need the training intake. Call us if you're unsure.

01Everyone

Liability waiver & release

Assumption of risk and release for anyone who rides, handles, or is around the horses — visitors included. Required before you get near a horse. Minors are covered by a parent or guardian signature.

Open form
02Boarders

Boarding agreement

The board contract — horse details, your info, what's included, payment terms, and the care/custody terms. Signed once when your horse moves in.

Open form
03Riders

Lesson registration & waiver

Sign up a rider — kid or adult. Experience, helmet acknowledgment, and the liability release all in one. One per rider, then you're set for the season.

Open form
04Minors

Youth & camp consent

For day camp, youth horsemanship, and homeschool programs — parental consent, medical info, emergency contacts, and authorization to treat. One per child, per season.

Open form
05Training

Training intake agreement

Drop a horse for 30/60/90-day training or colt starting. Goals, history, vices, vet/farrier, and the training terms — including the daily-care and release language.

Open form
06Owners

Emergency vet authorization

Tells us what to do if your horse needs a vet and we can't reach you — who to call, your spending limit, and standing instructions. Keep it on file for every horse.

Open form
07Owners

Horse health & Coggins record

Vaccinations, negative Coggins, deworming, shoeing schedule, feed, and any conditions or medications. So we care for your horse right from day one.

Open form
08Optional

Photo & media release

Say yes or no to us sharing photos and video of you, your rider, or your horse on our website and social. Totally optional — opting out changes nothing else.

How it works

Sign once.
We keep it
filed.

Every form goes straight to Meg the second you submit it, and a copy is filed and backed up automatically — no paper to lose, no folder to dig through.

You can save your own copy at the end of each form (your phone or browser will save it as a PDF).

These forms exist because of our insurance and Tennessee's equine-activity law — they protect you, your horse, and us. The warning notice required by TCA Title 44, Chapter 20 appears on every one.

Questions about any form? Call or text (423) 607-5656 — we'll walk you through it.
