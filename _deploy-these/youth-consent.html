Consent, authorization & release

As the parent or legal guardian of the child named above, I agree:

1. Permission to participate. I give permission for my child to take part in equine activities, riding, and horsemanship at Gracewood Acres.

2. Inherent risk & release. I understand equine activities are inherently dangerous and that horses are unpredictable; injury or death can occur despite supervision. On behalf of my child, myself, and our heirs, I release, waive, and hold harmless Gracewood Acres, its owners, staff, and volunteers from all claims arising from my child's participation, including ordinary negligence, to the fullest extent permitted by Tennessee law, and I indemnify them against claims arising from my child's participation. I understand the farm's liability insurance does not cover injury to participants.

3. Authorization to treat. If my child is injured or ill and I cannot be reached, I authorize Gracewood Acres to seek and consent to emergency medical, dental, or surgical treatment by a licensed provider, and I accept financial responsibility for that care.

4. Helmet & instructions. My child will wear a provided, approved riding helmet for all mounted activity and follow staff directions.

5. Governing law. This agreement is governed by Tennessee law; if any part is unenforceable, the rest stands.
