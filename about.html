Meet the
people who

A small crew runs a working horse farm in East Tennessee — hands-on, every day. Here's everyone, plainly.

The same people care for your horse every single day. The realness is the brand — so here's everyone, plainly.

[ MEG / starting a colt in the round pen ]

Meg

Owner · trainer · lead instructor

10+ years with horses. Harvard Business Analytics certificate. UT Extension Master Beef Producer. She starts the colts, works the problem horses, and teaches the lessons. Hands-on every day — not a manager who checks in by text.

[ BECCA / morning feed, every horse by name ]

Becca

Barn manager

She runs morning and evening feeding, daily horse care, and the youth program welcome desk. The horses genuinely like her more than they like most people — and she knows it.

[ ROBBIE / out on the fence line ]

Robbie

Facilities

Twenty acres of fence, pasture, equipment, and barn don't maintain themselves. That's his lane — and the reason the water's always running and the fences are always standing.
