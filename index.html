The Gracewood difference

The same hands
on your horse,
every day.

We're not the fanciest barn in East Tennessee and we have zero interest in being. What we are is a tight crew that's hands-on every day. Meg trains and teaches. Becca runs the barn and knows every horse by name, quirk, and preference. Robbie keeps the fences standing, the pastures mowed, and the water running.

Your horse is not dropped off and checked on twice a week. It's fed, handled, and looked at by the same people, every single day.

That's it. That's the pitch. — The Gracewood crew
