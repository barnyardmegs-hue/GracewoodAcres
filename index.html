About · where we're at

A small barn,
still being
built.

Gracewood is one trainer, a few horses, and a community growing one rider at a time. We don't run a show pipeline. We don't chase certifications. We put actual hours in the saddle, listen to what the horse and the rider are telling us, and try to do a little better than yesterday.

A bigger place is the biggest thing on our horizon — room for a hay field, woods to ride, and a proper working farm taking shape. The kind of place that earns the name Acres.

What we do

Three programs
that matter.

We keep the list short on purpose. Most of our days are spent putting time on horses, coaching kids and adults, and getting the next thing ready — not selling polish.

Training

The biggest piece of what we do. Green colts started right, broke horses kept supple, and the ones in between brought back to themselves.
